About Grand Palms

Our Story · Our People · Our Promise

Our Story

Founded in 1987, Grand Palms Hotel began as a vision of two brothers — Marco and Luca Ferretti — who believed that hospitality was not just a service, but an art form. What started as a modest 30-room boutique hotel on Miami's beachfront has grown into one of the most celebrated luxury properties in the Americas.

Over four decades, we have welcomed presidents, artists, musicians, and families from every corner of the world.

Our Mission

Our mission is simple: to create moments that guests will treasure for a lifetime. Every element of the Grand Palms experience — from the Egyptian cotton sheets to the hand-crafted cocktails at our rooftop bar — is thoughtfully designed to exceed expectations.

We invest deeply in our people, our community, and our environment. We are proud to be a certified Green Key property.

Our Values

Excellence

We never settle for anything less than the finest in every detail, from cuisine to customer care.

Warmth

Every guest is treated as family. Genuine warmth and hospitality are the cornerstones of our culture.

Integrity

We are honest, transparent, and fair — with our guests, our staff, and our partners.

Innovation

We embrace new ideas while honouring the timeless traditions of fine hospitality.
